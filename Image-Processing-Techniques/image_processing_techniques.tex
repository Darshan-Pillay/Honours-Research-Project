\paragraph{Stomotal Conductance Thermal imaging} \hfill \\ 
 A key phenotypical parameter to measure in these contexts is stomotal conductance. Plants are cooled by transipiration the more gas that is diffused through their stomota, when a plant has inadequate amounts of water its temperature rises as transipiration decreases as a stress response to water scarcity \cite{martinMethodsDeterminingWhen2009}.
