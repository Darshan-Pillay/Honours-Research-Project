\paragraph{Foundations of image acquistion in plant phenotyping}
\begin{itemize}
	\item  measure a phenotype quantitatively through the interaction between light and plants such as reflected photons, absorbed photons, or transmitted photons. Each component of plant cells and tissues has wavelength-specific absorbance, reflectance, and transmittance properties  \cite{liReviewImagingTechniques2014}
	\item  For example, chlorophyll absorbs photons primarily in the blue and red spectral region of visible light  \cite{liReviewImagingTechniques2014}
	\item  Imaging at different wavelengths is used for different aspects of plant phenotyping  \cite{liReviewImagingTechniques2014}.
	\item  Imaging techniques are very helpful for detecting these properties, especially for those that cannot be seen by the naked eye \cite{liReviewImagingTechniques2014}
\end{itemize}

\paragraph  {Image acquistion methodologies in plant phenotyping}

\subparagraph{Thermal imaging sensors} \hfill
\begin{itemize}
	\item Conceptual basis of this technique \cite{prakashTHERMALREMOTESENSING2000}
	\item Have been used to measure plant water status. 
	\item Used in Irrigation scheduling.
	\item By inferring transpiration rate of leaves which is governed by stomoatal conductance.
	\item Stomotal conductance can indicate drought, pathogens, and also soil salinity. 
	\item Have also been used for yield predictions \cite{ishimweApplicationsThermalImaging2014} 
\end{itemize}

\subparagraph{Hyperspectral sensors} \hfill
\begin{itemize}
	\item Based on the principles of spectroscopy. Can detric biochemical, morphological and physilogical traits of plants. Has been used to detect drought stress. Also used to detect water stress, and pathogens. Different reflectance can tell us about different things. Certain band tells us about water stress, some can tell us about clholrphyll content. \cite{slatonEstimatingNearinfraredLeaf2001}.
	\item Conceptual basis and applications \cite{saricApplicationsHyperspectralImaging2022}
	\item Crop yield
	\item Biotic and abiotic factors
\end{itemize}

\subparagraph{Tomography} \hfill
\begin{itemize}
	\item three-dimensional visualization of plant internal structures, root systems, and physiological processes
	\item Based on principles of xray cts, moving sensor takes planar cross sections to 3d visialize plant
	\item Drought stress \cite{vadezLeasyScanNovelConcept2015}
	\item Yield Estimations 
	\item has references for usages \cite{paulusMeasuringCrops3D2019}
\end{itemize}

\subparagraph{Flouresnce sensors} \hfill
\begin{itemize}
	\item Conceptual basis of this technique \cite{tanFarredLightRegulator2022a}
	\item Predict metabolism using photosynthesis which tells us about growth status
	\item Any stress or disturbance can indirectly or directly affect plant metabolism, so chlorphyll content canbe us used as a measure to detect these things like pathogens, light shortage, nitrogen deficiency, water stress etc  \cite{lichtenthalerRoleChlorophyllFluorescence1988}
	\item In optimal conditions, very little heat is emitted in photosynthesis so in certain near infrared emission should be low
	\item Acitinic light involved here?
\end{itemize}
